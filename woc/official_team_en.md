# Regulations for Applying as an Official Representative Team in the CYBER-ICE.RU League

## 1. General Provisions

These regulations define the procedure for applying to recognize an in-game team as the **official representative** of an organization (including, but not limited to, real-life hockey clubs) within the CYBER-ICE.RU league.

The application must be submitted by the team captain and must be approved by the organization’s management, as well as confirmed by the League Administration.

For clarity, the word **organization** in the following sections will refer to a real hockey club.  
However, all rules and procedures described in this document apply to **any type of organization** that may be represented in the league.

## 2. Application Procedure

**2.1. Approval from the Real Club’s Management**  
A team captain who wishes to affiliate their in-game team with a real hockey club must:

- Contact the management of the real club (a legal entity or an authorized representative)  
- Inform them about the intention to use the club’s name, logo, and other branding elements in the CYBER-ICE.RU league  
- Obtain preliminary consent to use the club’s branding

**2.2. Notification to the CYBER-ICE.RU League Administration**  
After receiving consent from the club’s management, the team captain must:

- Contact the CYBER-ICE.RU League Administration via official communication channels (Telegram or email)  
- Report that consent has been granted and provide the contact details of the club’s representative for verification

**2.3. Verification Request Sent by the League Administration**  
The CYBER-ICE.RU League Administration will send an official verification request to the real club’s email address.

**2.4. Response from the Real Club**

The management of the real hockey club must send an official reply to the request from the League Administration.  
Below is a sample response:

---

**Subject:** Authorization to Use Branding in CYBER-ICE.RU League

Hello,

By this letter, **[official name of the organization]** grants permission to the captain of the virtual team *[name of the cyber team]* in EA SPORTS NHL 25, *[full name of the team captain]*, to use the official logo of our organization, or another logo provided by our organization for *[name of the cyber team]*, as the team emblem:

1) On the website https://cyber-ice.ru  
2) In the official Telegram chat [chat link: https://t.me/cyber_ice_ru]

We also confirm that the virtual team *[name of the cyber team]* is our official representative and may receive **verified status** on the CYBER-ICE.RU League website.

The use of the logo and any other branding must be done **without graphic changes** and must follow all rules related to **intellectual property protection**.

If you have any questions, please contact us by email: [insert contact email] or phone: [insert phone number].

---

## 3. Application Review by the League Administration

After receiving confirmation from the real club, the CYBER-ICE.RU League Administration will:

- Verify the accuracy of the submitted information  
- Add the team to the list of official representatives  
- Publish the club’s logo on the website and official league channels

## 4. Responsibilities of the Team Captain

The team captain must:

- Follow the rules for using the branding of the real club  
- Not change the logo or team name without approval from the club’s management  
- Immediately inform the League Administration of any changes in the affiliation status

## 5. Final Provisions

- If the branding rules are violated, the League Administration reserves the right to remove the official representative status  
- All disputes will be resolved through communication between the team captain, the real club’s management, and the League Administration

---

**Note:**  
The CYBER-ICE.RU League Administration reserves the right to update these regulations.  
All participants will be informed, and the latest version will be published in the official GitHub repository.
